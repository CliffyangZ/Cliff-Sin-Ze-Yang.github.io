I.

About

I am an undergraduate studying Computer Science, drawn to the frontier where intelligence, physics, and embodied autonomy converge. My curiosity spans the full spectrum — from the abstract mathematics of quantum information theory to the real-world constraints of systems that move, see, and act.

I believe the most interesting problems today live at intersections: What does it mean for a machine to reason? How does quantum mechanics redefine the limits of computation? How can an autonomous agent bridge symbol and sensation? I am here to explore that synthesis.
